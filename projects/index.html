Volunteer Booking Portal

Turning a variable process into something clear and trackable.

Problem:

Volunteers and coordinators struggled with a manual, confusing booking process that made scheduling difficult and tracking impossible.

Solution:

I simplified the process by launching the booking portal, reworking how data was entered and displayed. The goal was usability — clear visuals, fewer steps, more feedback.

Impact:

Usage increased almost immediately. It now produces monthly summaries that reveal patterns in bookings, responses, and timing — insights we never had before.

Sunvote Audience Response System

Bringing interactive feedback back to life.

Problem:

Sunvote, SAAMBR asset, had fallen out of use because it was seen as too complex to manage.

Solution:

I took on the challenge of learning, testing, and documenting the system. I also created a simple manual and presentation to make it accessible for staff and volunteers.

Impact:

Along with Formal Education, we successfully reintroduced Sunvote for several high school courses, turning it into a practical, quick-feedback tool pre and post course evaluations.

Education Volunteers Group Minutes

From note-taking to strategic communication.

Problem:

Meeting minutes were often functional but lacked clarity and cohesiveness.

Solution:

As secretary, I restructured minutes to capture outcomes and provide a smooth reading experience, creating a record that guided next actions and improved transparency.

Impact:

Minutes were easy to follow resulting in clear messages being delivered to volunteers and aligning with the group’s work with wider goals.

Data Storytelling Report (Pilot)

Transforming dashboard data into a one-page story.

Problem:

Data collected from volunteer systems sat unused, and leadership needed an easier way to see progress at a glance.

Solution:

I built a prototype that automatically generated a one-paragraph snapshot — “the story of the week” — from the dashboard data, summarizing trends in volunteer activity.

Impact:

This prototype showed how data could feed into communication outputs — quick stories that highlight volunteer impact and inform planning.
